Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Age, Income

Based on the plots we tried to find a correlation between Healthcare,age and Income across fifty states in the U.S.

The States of Massachusetts(MA),Vermont(VT) followed by Minnesota(MN) are better in healthcare and are on the top three with respect to age, poverty and income aspects.

The States of Texas(TX),Georgia(GA) followed by Mississippi(MS) lack healthcare and are on the bottom three with respect to age, poverty and income aspects.

Based on the data,the Obesity is at higher rate in the age range on 36-40 and the annual house income in the range $40,000 to $55,000.
A similar correlation is observed with Smoking percentage which is higher in the age range 36-42 and the annual house income in the range $40,000 to $55,000.
This shows a trend in lack of healthcare with the above age and income range.
